She remained calm <br>
through the chaos <br>
Her soul consistent <br>
<!-- split -->
through all the<br>
inconsistencies of life <br>
vibrantly colored<br>
in a monotonous world<br>
She never once doubted <br>
what she wanted <br>
and never once <br>
stopped believing in it <br>
.
