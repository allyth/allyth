About

Hey! My name's Ally, I'm 18 years old, and I live in the Chicago area. I love drawing, coding, web designing, photography, poetry, and tennis. I started drawing/painting when I was about 6 years old and have been doing it ever since. My work was featured at the Naperville Woman's Club's Art Fair and in local school's art fairs throughout all 4 years of high school. I was a member of the Naperville Fine Art League from 2015 to 2018 and I am a member of Arts DuPage Foundation (2016-present). Since 2015, I have developed an interest in photography and film making & editing and really like it. Some of my hobbies are: writing poetry, modeling, and playing the guitar.

As of 2019, I am continuing my education in the field of Computing and Digital Media at DePaul University, studying computer science. Starting July 2019, I am a student volunteer at the Center for Data Science at DePaul University where I'm currently exploring machine learning, computer vision & data mining.

To see more of my creative work, click here. You can contact me through my LinkedIn account.
