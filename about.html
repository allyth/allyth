Hi! My name's Ally, I'm 19 years old, and I live in the Chicago area. Starting 2019, I am an undergraduate student at DePaul University, majoring in Computer Science at the College of Computing and Digital Media. As of July 2019, I am studying machine learning, computer vision, and data mining at the Data Science Center of DePaul University and I am a member of the Visual Informatics and Data Analytics Group. Since February 2020, I work as a part-time Undergraduate Research Assistant at DePaul's Data Science Center; in addition, starting October 2020, I am the Student Assistant for the Associate Provost for Research.

Some of my hobbies include coding, drawing, film making/editing, photography, and tennis. I started drawing/painting when I was about 6 years old and have been doing it ever since. My work was featured at the Naperville Woman's Club's Art Fair and in local school's art fairs throughout all 4 years of high school. I was a member of the Naperville Fine Art League from 2015 to 2018 and of Arts DuPage Foundation from 2016-2019.
