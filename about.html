About

Hey! My name's Ally, I'm 16 years old, I am a high school student, and I live in the Chicago area. I love drawing, coding, photography, tennis, and bowling. I started drawing/painting when I was about 6 years old and have been doing it ever since. My work was featured in Naperville Women's Club's Art Fair in 2015, in the school's art fair, and I am a member of the Naperville Fine Art League as well as Arts DuPage. I am proud to have taken part in the Girls Who Code program and I am also doing digital drawing/graphics, with the intention to focus more on it in the years to come.

I started photography in 2015 and really like it. You can check out my work (and not only) on this website as well as on my NAL page. Some of my hobbies are: modeling, video making/editing and playing the guitar.

You can contact me through my LinkedIn account.
